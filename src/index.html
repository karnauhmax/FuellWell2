Frequently asked questions

  • What is FuelWell? What is it needed for?

    FuelWell is an additional fuel-system plug-in hardware device for a wide variety of gasoline and diesel internal combustion engines. FuelWell catalytic inside effect leads to complete fuel combustion, therefore reducing emissions of harmful substances such as CO- by 39%, CO2 – by 15%, NOx – by 35%, and PM(soot) – by 90%. Moreover, FuelWell reduces fuel consumption by at least 10%.

    What is the FuelWell operation principle?

    The FuelWell technology works on the principle of catalysis. When the fuel passes through the device’s internal cassette a catalytic process takes place, making diesel or gasoline fuel burn more efficiently and in full volume, as a result, emission of harmful substances such as CO, CO2, NOx, and PM are reduced by 39-15-35 90 %. FuelWell modifies the fuel molecules’ physical condition, the chemical composition of the fuel does not change.

    Does the FuelWell device change the chemical composition of the diesel and gasoline fuel?

    FuelWell modifies the fuel molecules’ physical condition, the chemical composition of fuel does not change.

    Does the FuelWell device require any service during its lifetime /operation resource?

    FuelWell device does not require any service during the operation, there are no replacement parts or consumables.

    Does the FuelWell device require a connection to the vehicle’s electrical network?

    FuelWell does not require a connection to a vehicle electrical network?

    What engines and vehicle types are FuelWell devices applicable for?

    We made FuelWell devices to suit every internal combustion engine specific parameter, by developing two types, one for diesel, and another for gasoline, as well as a wide model line for both types to show the best results on any transport, starting from a passenger car, 18-wheeler trucks, and agricultural techniques, ending with mining techniques, vessels, locomotives, and diesel generators. We field-tested FuelWell devices on more than 70 engine types.

    How differs the FuelWell model line? How to choose a suitable model?

    Different FuelWell models were developed for gasoline and diesel internal combustion engines with different productivity parameters and fuel consumption. To choose suitable FuelWell devices for your corporate fleet or personal use feel free to reach us via the contact forms on the website.

    Does the ecology efficiency of FuelWell devices proven by independent certificates and test reports?

    Fuel Well ecological efficiency is proven by the notified European Laboratory ITS (Warsaw, PL) regarding the WLTP cycle: report № 0527 / COS / 18. Fuel saving efficiency is proven by industry partners’ test reports which you may find on this website section named “FUEL Consumption EFFICIENCY

The supply kit consists of the FuelWell device itself, junction fuel pipes and hoses, special bracings and fastenings, an installation manual together with warranty document.
