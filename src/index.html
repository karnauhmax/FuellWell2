  • What is FuelWell? What is it needed for?

FuelWell operation reduces emissions of harmful substances such as CO- 39%, CO2 – 15%, NOx – 35%, and PM – 90% due to more complete combustion of fuel.     Does FuelWell change the chemical composition of the fuel?

    FuelWell content effect the physical condition, the chemical composition of the fuel does not change.

    What is the FuelWell operation principle?

    The FuelWell technology works on the principle of catalysis. When the fuel passes through the device and comes into contact with the internal special cassette a catalytic process takes place that allows the fuel to burn more efficiently and in full volume, as a result, CO, CO2, NOx, and PM emissions are reduced by 39-15-35 90 % of harmful substances, the effect on the physical condition, the chemical composition of the fuel does not change.
